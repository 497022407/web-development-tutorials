    Podiatrist

    Kirsty is a current member of both the Australian Podiatry Association and Sports Medicine Australia. She regularly participates in their ongoing education programs and seminars in order to maintain the highest standards of podiatry care that every patient deserves.

  • Dr Jenny Gunn

    General Practitioner

    Dr Jenny Gunn has been a GP for the last 18 years, with 6 of those working in a rural practice before moving to Adelaide. Dr Jenny practices in all aspects of Family Medicine, with a particular interest in skin cancer and chronic disease management.

    Podiatrist
